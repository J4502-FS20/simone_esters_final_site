College Twirlers: A University's Gem

Baton twirling is thought to be a dying art, but it is more popular than ever! With daring tosses and gymnastics elements, these are not your mom's majorettes!

Baton twirling was one of the most popular activities for women in the US prior to Title 9.Twirling is commonly known to be a simple halftime performance done at a football game, now college feature twirlers will twirl with fire and more.
These are often competitors who travel across the world to twirl against some of the top athletes of their kind globally to win national and world medals.
Every year there are contests held across the country, but only one twirler will win the title of College Miss Majorette of America... you can meet her on this site!
